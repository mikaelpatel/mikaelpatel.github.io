Benchmark support macros. Use in the form:

1 BENCHMARK("description") statement

Prints description message and time to execute the statement in micro-seconds. The maximum resolution depends on micros() and is typically 4 us (F_CPU=16 MHz). Message strings are stored in program memory.

Parameters
[in]msgdescription of the benchmark.
