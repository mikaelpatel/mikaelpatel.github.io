Detailed Description

Cosa Device Driver for DS1302, Trickle-Charge Timekeeping Chip.

Circuit

DS1302/RTC
+------------+
(VCC)---------------1-|VCC |
(GND)---------------2-|GND |
(D2)----------------3-|CLK |
(D3)----------------4-|DAT |
(D4)----------------5-|RST |
+------------+
