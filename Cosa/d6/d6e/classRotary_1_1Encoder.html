Detailed Description

Rotary Encoder using pin change interrupts. Handles half and full cycle detection. Will push an Event::CHANGE_TYPE with the direction of the change.

Circuit

KY-040 Rotary Encoder Module.

Rotary Encoder
+------------+
(PCIc)--------------1-|CLK |
(PCId)--------------2-|DT |
3-|SW (/) |
(VCC)---------------4-|VCC |
(GND)---------------5-|GND |
+------------+
