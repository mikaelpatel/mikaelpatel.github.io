Detailed Description

ERM1602-5 Series, Character Display Module, adapter with digital output pins.

Circuit

The serial 3-line SPI circuit for ERM1602-5.

ERM1602-5
+------------+
(GND)---------------1-|VSS |
(VCC)---------------2-|VDD |
3-|VO |
(D5)----------------4-|CS |
(D6)----------------5-|SCL |
(D7)----------------6-|DI |
7-|PS |
8-|RST |
(VCC)---------------9-|LED+ |
(GND)--------------10-|LED- |
+------------+
