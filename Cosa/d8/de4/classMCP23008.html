Detailed Description

Driver for the MCP23008 8-bit I/O Expander with I2C Interface and Interrupt.

Circuit

MCP23008
+------------+
(SCL)---------------1-|SCL VCC|-18--------------(VCC)
(SDA)---------------2-|SDA GP7|-17---------------(P7)
(A2)----[ ]---------3-|A2 GP6|-16---------------(P6)
(A1)----[ ]---------4-|A1 GP5|-15---------------(P5)
(A0)----[ ]---------5-|A0 GP4|-14---------------(P4)
(RST)---------------6-|RESET/ GP3|-13---------------(P3)
7-|NC GP2|-12---------------(P2)
(EXT)---------------8-|INT GP1|-11---------------(P1)
(GND)---------------9-|GND GP0|-10---------------(P0)
+------------+
