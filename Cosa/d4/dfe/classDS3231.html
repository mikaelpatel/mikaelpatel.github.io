Detailed Description

Driver for the DS3231, Extremely Accurate I2C-Integrated RTC/TCXO/Crystal.

Circuit

The Mini RTC pro module with pull-up resistors (4K7) for TWI signals.

Mini RTC pro
+------------+
1-|32KHz |
2-|SQW |
(A5/SCL)------------3-|SCL |
(A4/SDA)------------4-|SDA |
(GND)---------------5-|GND |
(GND)---------------6-|VCC |
+------------+
