Detailed Description

C0-C8 scale, suffix -s/is of sharp, and -s/es for flat, Scandinavian/Dutch naming convention.

Usage

Tone::play(Note::C4);

References

  1. Frequencies for equal-tempered scale, http://www.phy.mtu.edu/~suits/notefreqs.html
