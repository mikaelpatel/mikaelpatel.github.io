Detailed Description

Driver for the HMC5883L 3-Axis Digital Compass IC, a vector magnetometer.

Circuit

The GY-80 10DOF module with pull-up resistors (4K7) for TWI signals and 3V3 internal voltage converter.

GY-80
+------------+
(VCC)---------------1-|VCC |
2-|3V3 |
(GND)---------------3-|GND |
(A5/SCL)------------4-|SCL |
(A4/SDA)------------5-|SDA |
6-|M-DRDY |
7-|A-INT1 |
8-|T-INT1 |
9-|P-XCLR |
10-|P-EOC |
+------------+
