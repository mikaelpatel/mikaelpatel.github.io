Detailed Description

template<BOARD::pin_t RST_PIN = BOARD::D6, BOARD::pin_t SCE_PIN = BOARD::D5, BOARD::pin_t DC_PIN = BOARD::D4, BOARD::pin_t SDIN_PIN = BOARD::D3, BOARD::pin_t SCLK_PIN = BOARD::D2>
class PCD8544< RST_PIN, SCE_PIN, DC_PIN, SDIN_PIN, SCLK_PIN >

Device driver for PCD8544 48x84 pixels matrix LCD controller/driver. Default pins are for LCD4884 Shield.

Parameters
[in]RST_PINreset pin (default BOARD::D6).
[in]SCE_PINscreen chip enable pin (default BOARD::D5).
[in]DC_PINdata/command select pin (default BOARD::D4).
[in]SDIN_PINscreen data pin (default BOARD::D3).
[in]SCLK_PINscreen clock pin (default BOARD::D2).

Circuit

PCD8544 is a low voltage device (3V3) and signals require level shifter (74HC4050 or 10K resistor).

PCD8544/LCD
+------------+
(D6)---------[ > ]--1-|RST |
(D5)---------[ > ]--2-|SCE |
(D4)---------[ > ]--3-|DC |
(D3)---------[ > ]--4-|SDIN |
(D2)---------[ > ]--5-|SCLK |
(3V3)---------------6-|VCC |
(GND)--------[220]--7-|LED |
(GND)---------------8-|GND |
+------------+
(D7)------------------BL (optional)

References

  1. Product Specification, Philips Semiconductors, 1999 Apr 12. https://www.sparkfun.com/datasheets/LCD/Monochrome/Nokia5110.pdf
